Errata Found in Original Slides

This study tool's verification pipeline caught and corrected the following errors in the lecture slides:

  1. Ch 2, p.24: Z-Y-Z Euler angles degenerate case ($\beta=180\degree$) — sign error in $\gamma$ formula
  2. Ch 4, p.25: Pieper's method z-equation — sign error in $k_2$ coefficient
  3. Ch 5, p.20: Jacobian ${}^3 J_3$ entry — $L_2 \sin\theta_2$ should be $L_1 \sin\theta_2$

All corrections are documented in the respective chapter pages with links to the original PDF.
